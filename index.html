Humanitarian Needs:

State of Play

The Good News:

We are doing more for more people than ever before.

The Bad News:

The humanitarian system is under unprecedented strain.

In just over a decade, the number of
people in need of aid has almost doubled.
2015 opened with 73 million people
in 22 countries needing assistance.

Six months later, we are calling for help to
support an astounding 78.9 million people.
We need $18.8 billion to help them.

In many countries, violence and insecurity
continue to cause massive internal
and cross-border displacement.

In 2015 alone,

700,000 people have been newly displaced.
